Hi, I'm Maricela!

PR Professional - Digital Marketing - Data Enthusiast

“Do we know what’s the latest?”
You sure will. Whether it is in English or Spanish, I’ve had the opportunity to keep my teams updated with the latest and most important social and traditional conversation during several issues and crisis situations, ranging from natural gas explosions to product recalls and labor issues. As a Senior Account Executive in the Public Affairs & Crisis practice at BCW, I specialize in crisis social and traditional media monitoring, English and Spanish content creation, social media marketing, and strategy and website maintenance efforts. I joined BCW as an intern, after graduating from the University of Oklahoma. Since then, I’ve shared my expertise with a range of clients in industries such as oil and gas, technology, automobile and government. In the past, I served in the former CITGO account as the LATAM trends and issues monitor and managed the digital strategy for Dallas Water Utilities, spearheading a bilingual strategy to reach the Spanish-speaking population for the latter. Currently as a bilingual professional, I coordinate national and LATAM-based media outreach and event planning on behalf of the Port of Brownsville and help manage crisis response efforts for clients within the agency, such as FedEx Express LATAM and Kimberly-Clark. Additionally, I also keep the American Chemistry Council and Dow accounts updated with the latest industry trends while managing grassroots efforts on behalf of Ford.
